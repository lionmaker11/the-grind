- **Receivership sale**: Frank R. Simon of Simon PLC (Bloomfield Hills, 248-720-0290) is the court-appointed receiver marketing **14 buildings** from the portfolio for sale (LoopNet listing ID 32148007, simonattys.com/palmer-park-apartment-complex-portfolio). Wayne County tax auction (Sept 2025) saw zero bids on several properties; remaining 5 are going to a second auction.
- **Why this matters for 708 Pallister**: The HDC rehabilitation-treatments discussion is almost certainly about how much renovation flexibility CoreVest/the eventual buyer will get on the distressed Palmer Park apartment buildings. **Any precedent HDC sets here applies to 708 Pallister too.** If HDC loosens interpretation of contributing/non-contributing elements for those 14 buildings, that's a tailwind. If they tighten, it's a headwind.
