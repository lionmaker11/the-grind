# Skill: Meeting Prep

## Trigger
T.J. says "prep for [meeting/call] with [person]" or chained from morning-brief when meetings detected.

## Process
1. Look up `people/[person].md` in vault for full context history.
2. Check relevant `projects/` folder for anything involving that person.
3. Check `decisions/` for any recent decisions involving that person.
4. Check `action-items/` for any open items assigned to or involving that person.
5. Compile:
   - Last interaction summary
   - Open action items (theirs and T.J.'s)
   - Patterns to be aware of
   - Suggested talking points
   - Any decisions pending that involve them
6. Push to Telegram.

## Output Format
Bullet points. Brief. One phone screen. T.J. should be able to glance at this 2 minutes before the call.
